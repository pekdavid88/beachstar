About us

A well-established, family-run hotel.

Set in a peaceful area a five-minute walk from Sidari's main street, Beach Star Hotel sits just across from the beach with its own pool and plenty of free sunbeds. Food is served either at the hotel or poolside across the road. If you're after a relaxing break rather than a busy resort, this is built for that.

PoolSwimming pool with a dedicated children's pool and free sunbeds & umbrellas.
BeachBeach sunbeds & umbrellas a short stroll away, across from the hotel.
Food & drinkBar, bar-restaurant, pool bar and pool snack bar.
FamilyBaby chairs at the restaurant, a children's pool, daily maid service.
LeisureBilliard table and a small library / book exchange.
